Classical Music Appreciation For Beginners

By classical music I mean "precise music", which includes music from the Baroque era, the classical era, the romantic era, the impressionist era, etc. It's easier to appreciate a piece if you know the meaning/story/picture connected to it. Here are some examples.

Beethoven: Piano Sonata No.17 in D Minor "Tempest"

Debussy: La Neige Danse, from "Children's Corner"

Chopin: Piano Sonata No.2 in B-Flat Minor, 3rd Movement "Marche Funè bre"
