I am a PhD candidate in Mechanical & Aerospace Engineering at University of California, San Diego. I received my B.S. degree at University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) in 2013, with a major in Chemical Engineering and a minor in Mathematics.

My current research area is stochastic control theory and its application to physics (e.g. Schrödinger equation). I am also interested in application of stochastic processes in biology (chemical reation networks, tumor growth, etc.)
