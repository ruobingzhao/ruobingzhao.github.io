Welcome

Ruobing Zhao

On Aug 30th, 2019, I successfully defended my Ph.D. Dissertation on stationary-action stochastic control representation of the Schrödinger initial value problem at University of California, San Diego (UCSD). I received my B.S. degree at University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) in 2013 and my M.S. degree at University of California, San Diego in 2015.
My research interests include stochastic optimal control and its application, dynamical systems, and stochastic differential equations.
